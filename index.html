Our Services

Comprehensive solar energy solutions tailored to your needs

Utility-Scale & Commercial Solar Solutions

End-to-end solar power systems for government buildings, industrial zones, and large commercial developments.

  • Site assessment and feasibility studies
  • System engineering and technical design
  • Procurement of Tier-1 solar equipment
  • Installation and commissioning
  • Grid-tied, hybrid, and off-grid solutions
  • Long-term operation and maintenance

Solar Equipment Rental Services

Flexible rental solutions for temporary, mobile, or project-based power requirements.

  • No upfront capital expenditure (CAPEX)
  • Short-term and long-term contracts
  • Rapid deployment and scalability
  • Full maintenance and support included
  • Mobile and modular solar units
  • Battery energy storage systems (BESS)

Operation & Maintenance
